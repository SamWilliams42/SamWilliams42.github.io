MOVIE AUDIO REPLACEMENT

This was a group project with Nathan Roldan and Gianna Gonzalez. We took a scene from the movie "A Quiet Place 2" and created entirely new audio for the scene using ADR, Foley, and sound effects from the Soundly library.

AUDIO AS A STORY

For this project my objective was to design audio to fit an image. I chose to tell a story with my audio, and used the Soundly and SoundIdeas sound libraries and some Foley to create it.
